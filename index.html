About this Project

This is a website that will lead to a game called "Jitterbug Cafe: Mini Coffee Shop Simulator". In this game, people will create an account in order to play the game. The game will have 5 levels (for now) and will increase with difficulty. There will be a tutorial page that will not garner a score. The score for the other levels will accumulate and give a play a rank based on the total score (all 5 levels combined). Ranks A-D. Essentially a leaderboard that will display other's scores including your own. This score will be reset every week essentially. Click "About" to see bug report.
