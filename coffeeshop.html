Game Rules & Instructions

These are the basic rules of the game! Follow them carefully!

  1. This game requires you to be signed in to play and record scores.
  2. There are 5 levels to this game, with each harder than the last.
  3. You will have a time limit of 5 minutes, which decreases by 30 seconds every level.
  4. The complexity of the drink increases with level as well.
  5. The goal is to make as many drinks (correctly) as you can within the time limit.
  6. For every correct cup made, you gain 50 points. Incorrectly subitted cups are -20 points.
  7. You will have an opportunity to fix inccorrect drinks.
  8. When the level is finished, you can either restart the level, or move on to the next.
  9. Only highest score achieved at end of level will remain regardless of #of attempts.
  10. When all levels are finished, total score will be determined, and rank given based on score.
  11. You can change this score/rank by improving the scores of each level.
  12. Your score and rank, along with your name will be posted on the leaderboard.
  13. Only top 5 will be featured on the home page.
  14. Leaderboard resets every week.
